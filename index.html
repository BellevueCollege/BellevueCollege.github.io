Who we are

The department of Technology Development & Communication (colloquially known as DevCom) is responsible for designing, implementing and maintaining technology-related solutions which improve the experiences of students, faculty, staff and the general public who use college services. We are part of the Information Services division, which oversees the college's technology plan and resources.

Core values

Transparency over Secrecy

  • Project status is publicly available
  • Bugs can be publicly submitted and viewed
  • Documentation is available when it does not pose a security risk
  • Future plans/goals are clearly documented and publicly available

External Collaboration over Isolation

  • External entities outside of the team can use and interact with our tool-set
  • Bugs can be assigned to and resolved by external entities as needed
  • Source code management can be interacted with externally as needed
  • Documentation can be contributed to externally as needed

Modularity Architecture over Monolithic

  • Tools focus on a specific function
  • Tools can integrate with other tool sets
  • Tools provide functionality that allows them to be extended

Agile Software Development Methods over Others

  • Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
  • Working software over comprehensive documentation
  • Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
  • Collaboration-driven design over Research-driven design (Agile UX vs Lean UX)
  • Responding to change over following a plan

Technological Diversity over Conforming

  • Tools provide platform equality
  • Platform does not dictate tools
  • Tools do not dictate platform

Internet Accessible over Internally Secure

  • Tools and environments are accessible directly from the Internet vs requiring VPN whenever possible
